# 1. What is CodeLogic?

CodeLogic is a website that helps people learn programming by playing short quizzes. It looks like a pixel-art game.

## The Basic Idea

You pick a topic (like HTML, Python, CSS, or JavaScript). The topic has **15 levels**. Each level has a short lesson and then about 10 quick quiz questions. You get points for right answers. You lose a heart for wrong ones. Finish all 15 levels and you get a certificate.

## Types of Questions

There are four kinds of questions:

- **Multiple choice** — pick the right answer from 4 options.
- **Find the error** — find the broken line of code.
- **What's the output** — say what the code will print.
- **Fill in the blank** — put the missing word in the code.

## Game Rules (short version)

| Thing | What it means |
|---|---|
| **XP** | Points you earn for right answers. |
| **Level** | Goes up as you earn XP. 500 XP per level. |
| **Hearts** | Your lives. Start with 10. Lose 1 per wrong answer. You get a new heart every 2 minutes. |
| **Streak** | How many days in a row you've played. Miss a day and it goes back to 1. |
| **Stars** | 1 star to pass a level, 2 stars for 80% or higher, 3 stars for 100%. |
| **Certificate** | You get one when you finish all 15 levels of a topic. |
| **Leaderboard** | Top 15 players by XP. |

## Who It's For

- People learning to code who want a fun way to practice.
- Teachers who want an easy tool for students.
- Admins who want to add new quizzes without writing code.

## Other Features

- **Learning Resources** — a section with free PDF guides you can read.
- **Profile** — pick one of 5 avatars, set a display name and a short bio.
- **Music and sound** — background music and sound effects you can turn on or off.
- **Admin panel** — a website admins use to add topics, questions, and lessons.
