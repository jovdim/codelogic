# 12. User Guide

How to actually use the site, for players.

## Making an Account

1. Go to the site and click **Register**.
2. Pick an email and a username (3+ letters/numbers/underscore).
3. Pick a strong password.
4. Check your email for a confirmation link. Click it.
5. Go to **Login** and sign in.

If the email doesn't come, go back to the login page — there's a **Resend** button.

## The Dashboard

After you log in, you land on the **Dashboard**. You'll see:

- Your avatar, level, and XP bar.
- How many hearts you have.
- Your streak (days in a row you've played).
- **Daily Challenges** — 3 small goals per day. Finish them for bonus XP.
- **Recent Activity** — your last few quizzes.
- **Topics** — jump into a topic and start playing.

## Playing a Quiz

1. Click **Play** in the sidebar.
2. Pick a category (like Frontend).
3. Pick a topic (like JavaScript).
4. Click a level.
5. Read the lesson slides (click Next).
6. Answer the questions.
7. See your result at the end.

### Rules

- You need **50% or higher** to pass a level.
- Passing unlocks the next level.
- Perfect score (100%) → **+50 bonus XP**.
- No hearts lost → **+25 bonus XP**.
- 3 stars = 100%, 2 stars = 80% or higher, 1 star = passed.

## Hearts

- You start with **10 hearts**.
- Lose 1 for each wrong answer.
- You get a new one every **2 minutes**.
- At 0 hearts, you can't start a new quiz until one comes back.
- The sidebar shows a countdown to the next heart.

## Streaks

- Playing any day counts.
- Miss a day, your streak resets to 1.
- Your longest streak stays saved.

## Certificates

Finish all 15 levels of a topic → you get a certificate. See them at **/certificates**.

Each certificate has:

- The topic name
- Stars earned
- Completion date
- A unique code (can be used to verify)

Click a card to open a printable version.

## Leaderboard

Top 15 players by XP. Visit **/leaderboard** to see it.

## Learn (PDFs)

Visit **/learn** for free PDF guides. You can search, and filter by category or language.

## Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to:

- Change your display name (once every 3 days)
- Change your bio
- Pick a different avatar (1–5)
- Change your password
- Delete your account

## Music and Sound

There's a speaker button in the top bar. Click to turn music on/off. Sound effects (right/wrong, level complete) play on their own.

Your music choice is remembered across pages.

## Tips

- Read the lesson slides. They teach the answers.
- If you run out of hearts, switch to a topic you know well, or take a break.
- Daily challenges stack with normal XP — the fastest way to level up.
